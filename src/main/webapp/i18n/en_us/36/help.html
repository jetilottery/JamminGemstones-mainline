Jammin’ Gemstones

Discover subterranean treasure in Jammin’ Gemstones! Match 3 or more adjacent gemstones of the same color to create clusters. The clusters disappear and the score for each gemstone increases as new gemstones fall in from above. Win a prize when you collect enough gemstones of the same color. Trigger the Gem Rush Bonus when you collect 4 gold rings - you might even win a prize instantly! Collect 4 silver rings and trigger the Shuffle Bonus: the gemstones may shift position on the grid, where they may be randomly collected to create new clusters and even better chances at winning a prize!

How to Bet

Ticket Cost

Displays the current ticket cost.

Press the left arrow (–) to decrease the ticket cost.

Press the right arrow (+) to increase the ticket cost.

Press BUY to deduct the ticket cost from the balance and play the game.

How to Play

The object of Jammin’ Gemstones is to match 3 or more adjacent gems of the same color.

3 or more adjacent gems or rings with the same center color gemstone on the grid create clusters. The clusters are removed and the score for each gem color increases accordingly. Collect the required number of any gem to win a prize. Multiple prizes are available for each gem color.

Collect 4 gold rings to trigger the Gem Rush Bonus and collect additional gems. Reveal a colorful gem symbol to win a prize instantly.

Collect 4 silver rings to trigger the Shuffle Bonus. Gems may change position on the grid, where they may be randomly collected or form new clusters.

Any awarded bonus game triggers when no clusters remain on the grid.

Dismiss the win message and view the game’s outcome by pressing the message.

Rules

Misuse or malfunction voids all pays and plays.

Bonus wins are added to total winning prize values.

Instant wins are added to total winning prize values.

Wins are shown in currency.

Player choices made during gameplay do not affect the game’s outcome.
